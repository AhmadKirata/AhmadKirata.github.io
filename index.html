A data analyst is responsible for collecting, processing, and performing statistical analyses on large datasets to extract insights and inform business decisions. They apply mathematical and statistical techniques to identify patterns and trends in data and use data visualization tools to communicate their findings to stakeholders. Data analysts also work on data cleaning and preprocessing to ensure that the data is accurate and reliable. They use tools such as Excel, SQL, Python, R, and data visualization software to perform their analyses. .
